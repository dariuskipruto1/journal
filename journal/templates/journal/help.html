Getting Started

Welcome to Journal Desk

Journal Desk is your personal digital journal with AI assistance, task management, and analytics. Here's how to get started:

  1. Create your first entry: Click "New Entry" from the dashboard or entries page.
  2. Add tasks: Include tasks in your entries to stay organized.
  3. Explore features: Check out the dashboard, analytics, and search features.
  4. Customize: Update your profile and preferences in the settings.

Navigation Guide

Use the smart navigation bar at the top to access different sections:

  • Dashboard: Overview of your activity and quick actions
  • Entries: View and manage all your journal entries
  • Tasks: Track and complete your tasks
  • Analytics: View insights about your journaling habits
  • Search: Find specific entries quickly

Troubleshooting

AI Features Not Working

If the AI assistant isn't responding:

  1. Ensure Ollama is installed and running
  2. Check that the llama2 model is downloaded: ollama pull llama2
  3. Verify the AI service is accessible at the configured URL
  4. Check the application logs for error messages

Weather Widget Not Showing

If weather information isn't displaying:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify the Open-Meteo API is accessible
  • Ensure location permissions are enabled (if using geolocation)
  • Check that weather widget is enabled in your profile settings

Task Reminders Not Working

For task reminder issues:

  • Ensure email alerts are enabled in your profile
  • Check that your email address is configured
  • Verify the task has a due date set
  • Check the management command is running: python manage.py send_task_alerts

Performance Issues

If the application is slow:

  • Check your database size and consider archiving old entries
  • Ensure proper indexing on database tables
  • Check server resources (CPU, memory)
  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Consider using a production database instead of SQLite

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes, your data is stored securely and is only accessible to you. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export all your journal entries and data from the Profile page. Data is exported in JSON format.

How do I backup my journal?

Use the export feature regularly to backup your data. You can also backup the database file directly.

Can I use Journal Desk offline?

The core functionality works offline, but AI features and weather data require an internet connection.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete all your data from the Profile page. Contact support if you need to completely remove your account.

Are there any limits?

There are no artificial limits on entries or tasks. Performance may vary based on your device and data size.
